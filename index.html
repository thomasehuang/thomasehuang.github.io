About Me

Hello! My name is Thomas E. Huang. I am a first-year PhD student at the Visual Intelligence and Systems group at the Computer Vision Lab, advised by Prof. Fisher Yu. Previously, I obtained my Master's and Bachelor's degrees from the EECS department at the University of Michigan, advised by Prof. Honglak Lee. My research interests are deep learning and its application to computer vision.
